About Us

The Accident Detection and Alert System using YOLO Model is an advanced AI-powered solution designed to enhance road safety by swiftly detecting accidents in real-time. Leveraging the YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection algorithm, the system efficiently identifies collisions from live video feeds with high accuracy. Upon detecting an accident, instant alerts are sent to emergency services and concerned authorities, ensuring rapid response and minimizing delays in critical situations. This cutting-edge system is ideal for highways, smart cities, and vehicle monitoring, offering a proactive approach to reducing fatalities and improving road safety standards through intelligent accident detection and timely alerts.
